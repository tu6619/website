One online definition describes a daydream as:

A series of pleasant thoughts about something you would prefer to be doing or something you would like to achieve in the future.

We are all 'guilty' of our thoughts wandering tangentially at some point or other during the day's work. In my view, these stray thoughts are not to be dismissed as distractions, but documented as pieces of inspiration. Interpreted through code, who knows what they may bear fruit to in the future...
